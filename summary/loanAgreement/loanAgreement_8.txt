On June 13, 2011, a loan agreement was made between Andrzej Nowak and Piotr Nowak. The agreement states that Andrzej Nowak (the lender) lends Piotr Nowak (the borrower) PLN 6,000 for the period from June 13, 2011 to September 13, 2011. The borrower agrees to pay interest at a rate of 2.5% per month, totaling PLN 450. The agreement also includes provisions for late payment and disputes.
