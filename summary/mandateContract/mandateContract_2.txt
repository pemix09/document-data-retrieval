The Mandate Contract between Zleceniodawca and Zleceniobiorca outlines the terms of a service agreement. The Zleceniobiorca agrees to perform certain work (usługi) for the Zleceniodawca, who will pay them a fee in złotys upon acceptance of the completed work. The Zleceniobiorca is not allowed to subcontract the work without the consent of the Zleceniodawca.
