On November 17, 1872, at 4:00 pm, a religious marriage was solemnized between Paweł Katas, born and residing in Goszczanów, son of Marianna Kalas and unknown father, aged 19 years and 8 months, and Marcjanna Pszczoła, born and residing in Goszczanów, daughter of Feliks and Marianna Ciupów, both deceased. The couple did not enter into a prenuptial agreement. The mother's permission was obtained orally. The marriage ceremony was performed by Father Benon Skurczyński, Vicar of the Goszczanowska Parish.
