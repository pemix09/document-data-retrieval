Dr. Mieczysław Groszek, born October 21, 1951, in Krosno. He holds a Master's degree in Labor Economics and Social Policy from SGPiS (now SGH) in Warsaw, and a Ph.D. in Economics in 1977. His professional career includes working as an assistant and lecturer at the Department of Economics at SGH, and later as an adjunct professor at the University of Cardinal Stefan Wyszyński. He has published numerous scientific and press articles on topics such as economic integration, entrepreneurship, capital market, and company valuation. In 1989-1990, he founded and served as vice-president of a consulting firm. Since 1990, he has been involved in banking.
