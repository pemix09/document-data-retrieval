The case concerns a dispute between Jan Kowal and Andrzej Naiwny regarding the ownership of a plot of land in Bolków, Lower Silesian Voivodeship. The plaintiff, Jan Kowal, is seeking the return of the land and its division from the defendant, Andrzej Naiwny. The dispute arose from an invalid sale agreement between the two parties. The case was heard by the Regional Court in Wrocław.
