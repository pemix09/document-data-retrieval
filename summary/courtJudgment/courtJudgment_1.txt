The Regional Court in Bydgoszcz issued a judgment on April 26, 2023, in case VII P 352/22. The court ordered the defendant to pay the plaintiff (company) 8,876.02 PLN as compensation for unjustified termination of employment contract without notice, along with interest from October 13, 2022, until payment. Additionally, the court ordered the defendant to return costs related to the proceedings, including attorney fees, with interest from the date of the judgment's enforcement until payment.
