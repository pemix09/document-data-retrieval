The Court of Appeal in Warsaw, V Civil Division, ruled in favor of Rzeczpospolita Polska against mBank S.A. The court judgment concerns the payment and settlement of a debt, with a total amount of 92,697.69 PLN and 24,480.67 CHF, plus interest from January 28, 2022, to the date of payment. Additionally, the court ordered mBank S.A. to pay Rzeczpospolita Polska 5,908.50 PLN for costs related to the lawsuit.
