The court judgment was issued on March 27, 2017, by the Sąd Rejonowy w Siemianowicach Śląskich I Wydział Cywilny. The case involved Niestandaryzowany Sekurytyzacyjny Fundusz Inwestycyjny Zamknięty and concerned a payment claim. The court dismissed the plaintiff's claims in their entirety and ordered the defendant to pay 2,417 PLN (two thousand four hundred seventeen Polish zlotys) for procedural costs.
