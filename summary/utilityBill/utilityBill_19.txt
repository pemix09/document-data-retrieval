The electricity bill for 2023 has been increased due to exceeding the limit. The assumed annual consumption is 2600-5000 kWh, with a penalty of 600 kWh at 3000 kWh. The total cost of energy and distribution in 2022 was 1971.94 PLN and 3564.67 PLN. With the VAT rate increased to 23%, the limit has been set at 2000 kWh. A bonus of 1000 PLN for electricity has also been taken into account.
